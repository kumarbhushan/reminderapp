About Suicide Call Back Service

Suicide Call Back Service is a 24 hour, Australia-wide service that provides free telephone, video and online counselling.

Our professionally trained counsellors have specialist skills in working with suicide-related issues and they can help you to work through the pain and distress you may be feeling.

You can call us anywhere and anytime on 1300 659 467.

Disclaimer

The ReMinder app has been developed to be part of your overall safety and mental health plan. It is not intended to be your only form of support. A trusted person in your life or a health professional can help you to develop your plan.

All content is intended for information and educational purposes only. Suicide Call Back Service assumes no responsibility for consequences resulting from the use of the information on this site.

If you are in immediate danger or are concerned about your safety in any way, call 000.

This app has been developed for people in Australia. If you are not in Australia and need help, please call your local emergency service.

About This App

The ReMinder app helps you to create a simple and convenient suicide safety plan on your phone. The app has been designed to be part of an overall mental health strategy and is not intended to be used in isolation.

The ReMinder app has been developed by On the Line, a social health business with over 55 years experience delivering mental health services, including Suicide Call Back Service.

K10 Assessment

The K10 assessment is a test to help you determine to what extent youve experienced depression or anxiety over the last month. The test may help you make an informed decision about seeking support or treatment if youve been experiencing depression or anxiety symptoms. The test has 10 questions and you will get a score at the end.

Please note:
The K10 is not a substitute for a case management plan with a counsellor.
The K10 is not a mental health diagnosis.

A K10 assessment should be completed once every six weeks at most. This will ensure it is as accurate as possible.
